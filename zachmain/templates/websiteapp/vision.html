Our Mission

Following the example of our Lord Jesus Christ, ZACH adopts the holistic approach to health and healing, encompassing principles of equity, quality, and accessibility. ZACH promotes the highest standard of health care among its members for the benefit of all people in Zimbabwe.

Our Vision

ZACH envisages a healthy society in which all people in Zimbabwe have access to sustainable high quality, holistic health care delivery.

ZACH Mandate

To assist and support member institutions in the provision of Quality Health Care Delivery to all Zimbabweans, especially to the poorest, vulnerable, and marginalized groups in society.

Our Belief and Faith

ZACH belief and faith comes from the teaching of our Lord Jesus Christ and is ecumenical coming from a range of Christian denominations and other faith sector actors hence our operational culture. Our belief is alleviating suffer and lives using the Holistic Approach to health and healing for the well-being of nations as well as capacitating and strengthening communities in addressing their vulnerabilities, Matthew “21:22″ And whatever you ask in prayer, you will receive, if you have faith”, and giving hope.
